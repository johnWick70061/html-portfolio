The Best Movies According to Anshuman

Top 3 Movies of all time

Inception

A mind-bending thriller where dreams within dreams blur the line between reality and illusion. Leonardo DiCaprio leads a team of extractors in a visually stunning, layered heist of the subconscious.

The Dark Night

Gotham's silent guardian faces chaos as the Joker tests the limits of morality and justice. With Heath Ledger's iconic performance, this dark, gripping tale redefined superhero cinema.

Interstellar

In a race to save humanity, astronauts journey through a wormhole searching for a new home. Christopher Nolan blends emotion and science in a cosmic epic exploring time, love, and survival.
